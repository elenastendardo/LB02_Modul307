Who am I and what do I do:

Hello everyone! I'm Elena Stendardo, I'm 17 years old and I live in Switzerland. I love travelling and photography. I decided to merge these 2 hobbies of mine and to start selling the pictures I take. They can be used for whatever you want, websites, books, articles, backgrounds... whatever you can think of! Just reach out to me using the form at the bottom of the page.

My Experience:

I've been travelling since I was a kid, thanks to my supporting family. I've been to so many places, and it's always difficult to remember everything I have seen. That's how taking pictures comes in handy! With the technological progress, we're now able to take good quality pictures everywhere anywhere without having to carry around a giant camera. I decided to share my pictures with the world, so that everyone could admire the beauty of the world.
